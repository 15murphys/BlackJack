Blackjack, also known to many as “twenty one” is a common casino played game. It has an unknown origin but referenced by a spanish author Miguel de Cervantes in 1601 , most known for writing “Don Quixote”. Blackjack is a card game that is played with 1 or more 52 card decks. It is played against the card dealer. trying to reach score of 21. You cannot get a score over 21. As the dealer gives you cards you either say. “ HIt me” or “Stand” Meaning to get another card or stay with the card total. If you get another card that has a value adding up higher than 21 then you bust and the dealer wins the round. The card values: Face cards-10 points, Ace-1 or 11 points, Numbers- Same value as number. Traditionally blackjack is played with 2-6 players but in our game things are played a little differently, played by one player at a time.
